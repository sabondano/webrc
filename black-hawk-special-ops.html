Officially licensed by:

The Sikorsky UH-60 Black Hawk is a four-bladed, twin-engine, medium-lift utility helicopter manufactured by Sikorsky Aircraft.

Sikorsky submitted the S-70 design for the United States Army's Utility Tactical Trasnsport Aircraft System (UTTAS) competition in 1972. The Army desginated the prototype as the YUH-60A and selected the Black Hawk as the winner of the program in 1976, after a fly-off competition with the Boeing Vertol YUH-61.
