Frequently Asked Questions

How do I power on my XDrone Zepto?
Turn on the XDrone by plugging in the battery.
How do I sync controller to my XDrone Zepto?
Turn on the XDrone Zepto. Then turn on the remote control and push the left joystick up and down several times until the propellers spin.
How do I make my XDrone Zepto hover?
To hover push left joystick up until desired height achieved. Gently adjust left joystick (pushing up or down accordingly) to maintain altitude.
How do I fly my XDrone Zepto forward/backward?
To fly forward, push right joystick up. To fly backward, push right joystick down.
How do I fly my XDrone Zepto left/right?
To fly left, push right joystick to the left. To fly right, push right joy stick to the right.
How do I make my XDrone Zepto rotate counter-clockwise/clockwise?
To turn counter clockwise push left joystick to the left. To turn clockwise push left joystick to the right.
I'm having a hard time flying my XDrone Zepto outdoors. What can I do?
Learn how to use L1 (power selector). L1 is the button on the top-left corner of the controller. Press L1 to change power level. Everyt time you press L1 you'll hear 1, 2, or 3 beeps. 1 beep = Power Level 1 (40% power), 2 beeps = Power Level 2 (70% power), and 3 beeps = Power Level 3 (100% power)
How do I do a back flip?
Use L1 to set power to 100%. Bring the XDrone Zepto to a hover at an altitude of 10 feet. Press and hold R1 (hold R1 until you hear a constant beep) , while holding R1 then push down on the right joystick. Let go of R1 and the right joystick after a back flip has been completed.
