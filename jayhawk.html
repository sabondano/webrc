The Sikorsky HH-60 Jayhawk is a multi-mission, twin-engine, medium-range helicopter operated by the United States Coast Guard.

First-flight was recorded on August 8th, 1989. It is used by the United States Coast Guard to rescue those in need in the most difficult conditions, they see cargo ships to port, and are our first line of defense in the face of everything from storms to oil spills. The Jayhawk is distinguished by it's speed, it's is capable of traveling at a top speed of 210 mph.
